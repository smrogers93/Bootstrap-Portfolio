About Me

Since then, I've found that I need to be working in an environment where I can be forced to learn new skills and to adapt constantly. When I fully realize my potential as a software engineer, there's definitely going to be a party involved.

Every day, I consume at least 3 cups of coffee in order to stay interested in anything longer than 10 seconds.

I'm naturally a Marvel fan because of my namesake "Captain America."
